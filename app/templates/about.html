About

We believe exercise is good. It's good for the mind, body and soul. We want to encourage everyone to make exercise a part of their lives.

But exercise is hard. We all lead busy lives. We're not all super heroes with the bodies of Greek Gods. We are normal people with jobs, families, commitments, finding it hard to make the time to exercise.

That's why we want to make it easy for you to track your goals and to log your exercises. It makes you feel good about yourself as you see the progress you're making week-on-week.

You don't need to join an expensive gym to exercise. In your own time, workout at home on your own or follow an instructor online. Be inspired by online sessions recommended by our user community. Bring the kids along.

Log My Exercise is here to help you exercise. To inspire, to track, to achieve exercise goals you never thought possible.
